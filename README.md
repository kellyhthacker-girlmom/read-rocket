# 🌟 Reading Stars - Kid-Friendly Reading Tracker

A fun, privacy-first web app that turns reading time into stars, badges, and team goals! Perfect for parents, teachers, and kids who want to make reading more engaging and rewarding.

## ✨ Features

### For Kids
- **Big Start Button** - Easy-to-use timer that tracks reading sessions
- **Quick Add** - Manually add minutes or pages read
- **Stars & Rewards** - Earn 1 star for every 10 minutes of reading
- **Daily Streaks** - Build reading habits with streak tracking
- **Fun Badges** - Unlock achievements like:
  - 📚 First Reader
  - 🌧️ Rainy Day Reader (3-day streak)
  - 🌙 Lights-Out Legend (7-day streak)
  - ⏰ Hour Master (60+ minutes in one session)
  - 🦸 Super Reader (50+ stars)
  - And more!
- **Team Goals** - See your class or family progress together
- **Anonymous Avatars** - Choose an animal (🦊🐻🦉🐱🐶🐰🐲🦄) and color theme

### For Parents & Teachers
- **Approve/Edit Logs** - Review and approve reading sessions
- **Custom Rewards** - Set rewards for reaching star milestones
  - Default: Movie Night (50 stars), Pick Dinner (100 stars), Extra Screen Time (200 stars)
- **Team Management** - Set monthly reading goals for the class/family
- **Privacy First** - No personal names required, only fun animal avatars

## 🚀 Getting Started

### Installation

1. **Clone or download** this repository
2. **Open `index.html`** in your web browser
3. That's it! No installation or server required.

### First Time Setup

1. **Choose Your Avatar**
   - Pick your favorite animal
   - Select a color theme
   - Click "Start My Reading Journey!"

2. **Start Reading**
   - Hit the big "Start Reading!" button when you begin
   - The timer will track your session
   - Click "Finish Reading" when done

3. **Or Quick Add**
   - Manually enter minutes or pages read
   - Click "Add Time" to log it instantly

## 📱 How to Use

### Kid View

**Timer Mode:**
- Click "Start Reading!" to begin tracking
- The timer shows minutes:seconds
- Click "Finish Reading" when done
- Earn stars automatically!

**Quick Add Mode:**
- Enter minutes read OR pages read
- Pages are estimated at 1.5 minutes per page
- Click "Add Time" to log

**Track Progress:**
- See your total stars and streak in the header
- View unlocked badges
- Watch the team goal bar fill up
- Check your recent reading log

### Parent View

Switch to Parent View to:

**Approve Logs:**
- Review pending reading sessions
- Approve, edit, or delete entries
- Adjust minutes if needed

**Manage Rewards:**
- Set custom star requirements
- Name your own rewards
- Track progress toward each reward

**Team Settings:**
- Adjust monthly goal for your class/family
- See all team members (anonymous avatars)
- View total team progress

## 🎯 Reading to Stars Conversion

- **1 star** = 10 minutes of reading
- **5 stars** = 50 minutes (almost an hour!)
- **10 stars** = 100 minutes (over 1.5 hours)

## 🏆 Badge System

Badges unlock automatically as kids read:

- **First Reader** - Complete 1st reading session
- **5-Star Reader** - Earn 5 stars
- **10-Star Champion** - Earn 10 stars
- **Rainy Day Reader** - Read 3 days in a row
- **Lights-Out Legend** - Read 7 days in a row
- **Hour Master** - Read 60+ minutes in one session
- **Bookworm** - Complete 20 reading sessions
- **Super Reader** - Earn 50 stars

## 🔒 Privacy Features

- **No personal information required**
- **No account creation**
- **No internet connection needed**
- **All data stored locally** in your browser
- **Anonymous avatars only** (animal + color)
- Perfect for classroom use with student privacy

## 💾 Data Storage

All data is stored in your browser's localStorage:
- Reading logs
- Stars and streaks
- Badge progress
- Team members
- Custom rewards

**Note:** Clearing browser data will reset the app. To backup, you can export localStorage manually.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Q: I lost my data after closing the browser**
- A: Make sure you're using the same browser and not in incognito/private mode

**Q: Timer isn't working**
- A: Refresh the page and try again. The timer doesn't persist through page reloads.

**Q: How do I reset everything?**
- A: Clear your browser's localStorage or delete the site data in browser settings

**Q: Can multiple kids use this on the same device?**
- A: Currently, it's single-profile. For multiple kids, use different browsers or browser profiles.
